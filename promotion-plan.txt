Client: Ko Matcha Bar

Promotion Plan – Ko Matcha Bar

Product/Service Being Promoted:
Ko Matcha Bar is a specialty café that serves high-quality matcha drinks, desserts, and light snacks in a modern, calming environment. The focus is on premium ingredients, aesthetic presentation, and a relaxing customer experience.

Target Audience:
The target audience is young adults aged 18–35, including students, creatives, and professionals who enjoy trendy cafés, aesthetic spaces, and healthier beverage options. This audience is highly active on social media and values visually appealing experiences.

Most Suitable Platforms:
Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest are the most suitable platforms. These platforms are ideal for sharing visually appealing content such as latte art, café interiors, and lifestyle imagery.

Proposed Hashtags:
#KoMatchaBar
#MatchaLovers
#CafeAesthetic
#SipAndRelax
#MatchaTime

Promotional Strategy:
The strategy is to focus on aesthetic, visually engaging content that highlights the café’s atmosphere and unique matcha creations. Posting short videos, behind-the-scenes clips, and customer experiences will attract attention, while encouraging customers to tag the café will help build organic reach and community engagement.

Note:
"Ko Matcha Bar" is a fictional business name used for the purpose of this academic project.
All branding, promotional material, and digital content were created solely for learning and demonstration.
